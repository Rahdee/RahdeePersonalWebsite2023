About Me

I am Rahdee Salie, a dedicated and hardworking UX/UI Developer, Graphic Designer, and Photographer. I am highly motivated and
thrive on taking up challenges, as they serve as a valuable learning experience for me.

I bring passion for my craft and commitment to delivering exceptional results in all my professional endeavors. I believe that every opportunity is a chance to learn and grow.

I obtained my N6 certificate in 2021 from the College of Cape Town, where I studied Art & Design at the College of Cape Town in 2019. While studying, I also gained working experience as a Retail Sales Consultant from 2019 - 2020.

In 2022 I enrolled at Samsung Future Innovation Lab at the
University of the Western Cape , whereby I obtained a Certificate
of Competency in Introduction to Multiplatform Social Development
and a Certificate of Competency for Digital Social Innovation for Impact.

While participating in the Samsung Future Innovation Lab course , my team and I created a mobile app that helps build a bridge between parents and educators, providing primary care givers with the information needed to help their kids.

I possess strong skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, .NET, C# and Microsoft PowerApps. These languages enable me to develop dynamic user interface. In addition to coding proficiency, I am well-versed in utilising various tools and technologies such as Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, Lightroom, and InDesign. These tools empower me to create visually captivating designs that effectively communicate the desired message and enhance user experiences.

My vision is to create a world where creativity knows no boundaries and is celebrated as a powerful force for positive change. In my personal and professional life, I strive to foster an environment of innovation, collaboration, and growth. I envision a workplace where diverse ideas are embraced, and individuals are encouraged to explore their full potential.

My mission is to inspire and empower others through creativity and innovation. I strive to use my skills and talents to bring positive change to the world. Whether it's through my work as a Developer, Graphic Designer and Photographer or any other endeavor I pursue. I aim to create meaningful and impactful experiences that touches people's lives. I am dedicated to continuous growth and learning, always seeking new opportunities to expand my horizons and make a difference. Ultimately, my mission is to leave a lasting legacy of inspiration, creativity, and positive impact on individuals and communities.
